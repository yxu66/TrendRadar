The new framework prohibits leverage lending exceeding collateral value and cash lending with Korean won repayment, while restricting exchanges to using only their own assets without third-party delegation. User protection measures include mandatory online education and qualification tests for first-time users, differentiated lending limits of 30-70 million won based on trading experience, pre-notification requirements for forced liquidation risks, and annual interest rate caps of 20%.
